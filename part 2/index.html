Welcome

Welcome to the birdwatching website! This site is designed for lovers of birds, from beginners to experts. Here you'll find information on getting started, photo galleries, gear reviews, and an active community forum.

Getting started

Birdwatching is a hobby that can be enjoyed by anyone, anywhere. All you really need to get started is a pair of eyes and some patience. However, the following gear can help you get more out of the experience:

  • A good pair of binoculars
  • A field guide to local birds
  • Comfortable, weather-appropriate clothing
  • A notebook to record your sightings

Photos
